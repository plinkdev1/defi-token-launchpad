# TRZ Tokenomics & Governance Model

## Overview

TRZ is a **governance and utility token** that enables participation in the Treezures protocol's decentralized operations. It is not a financial instrument, investment product, or claim on assets.

## Core Characteristics

### What TRZ Is
- **Governance Utility Token**: Grants access to protocol decision-making
- **Coordination Mechanism**: Enables community-led treasury and parameter management
- **Participation Token**: Required for governance voting and proposal submission
- **Voting Power**: Determines governance influence through decentralized voting

### What TRZ Is NOT
- **Financial Investment**: TRZ is not an investment security
- **Revenue Share**: Does not entitle holders to dividends or profit sharing
- **Ownership Stake**: Does not grant equity or ownership in Treezures Labs
- **Interest-Bearing Asset**: Does not generate interest or guaranteed returns
- **Equity Substitute**: Does not represent company shares or economic rights
- **Price Appreciation Vehicle**: Cannot be relied upon for financial gain

## Tokenomics Structure

### Supply & Distribution
- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 TRZ
- **Allocation Model**: Community-controlled, DAO-governed distribution
- **No Pre-mining**: Transparent issuance via governance decisions
- **Vesting Schedules**: All allocations subject to discretionary governance approval

### Governance Participation (Not Staking Rewards)
- **Lock Mechanism**: Users may participate in governance coordination tiers
- **Voting Weight**: Determined by governance tier participation, not financial returns
- **beTRZ (Escrowed)**: Escrowed TRZ from governance participation, with linear vesting schedules
- **Non-Custodial**: Users retain full custody and control

### Treasury Management
- **DAO-Controlled**: Community governance directs all treasury operations
- **Discretionary Allocation**: All fund deployments subject to governance votes
- **No Guaranteed Returns**: Treasury operations do not guarantee token holder returns
- **Transparent Reporting**: All treasury activities logged on-chain

## Governance Rights & Responsibilities

### Governance Functions
- **Proposal Submission**: TRZ holders may submit governance proposals
- **Voting Rights**: Vote on treasury allocations, parameter changes, and protocol upgrades
- **Parameter Adjustment**: Collectively adjust protocol parameters via governance
- **Launchpad Approvals**: Vote to approve new projects in the TRZ Launchpad

### No Financial Rights
- Governance participation does **not** entitle holders to:
  - Dividends or profit distributions
  - Revenue sharing or fee allocation
  - Claims on protocol assets or treasury
  - Guaranteed compensation or "rewards"

## Participation Model

### Governance Tiers
1. **Basic Tier**: Minimal governance participation
2. **Standard Tier**: Enhanced voting influence
3. **Premium Tier**: Maximum governance coordination

### Escrowed TRZ (beTRZ)
- **Linear Vesting**: TRZ vests linearly over 90–270 days post-unlock
- **Discretionary Allocation**: All beTRZ issuance determined by governance votes
- **Non-Guaranteed**: No assurance of token allocation or quantity
- **Cliff Period**: Initial lock period before vesting begins

## Risk Acknowledgment

**Users understand and accept:**
- TRZ is an experimental governance asset with no guaranteed value
- Participation is entirely voluntary and at user's own risk
- Governance decisions may negatively impact protocol utility
- Smart contract risks and technical failures are possible
- Regulatory treatment of governance tokens may change

## Compliance & Legal

TRZ is not intended or marketed as a financial instrument. It is a governance utility asset granting access to decentralized protocol operations. It provides no claim on assets, revenue, dividends, or profit. Users participate at their own risk and must comply with local jurisdictions.

**This tokenomics model has been designed to comply with regulatory safe-harbor provisions and should not be construed as financial advice.**
